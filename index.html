Agnes Lloyd-Platt is a fashion and beauty photographer and filmmaker based in London. Her work is characterised by positivity, colour, character and a graphic sensibility. Touching on these inspirations, Agnes embraces playfulness in her work and believes fashion photography should be uplifting and inclusive - “There are enough terrible things in the world for us to see. If you look at fashion imagery, it should be good to look at, and make you feel good.”
